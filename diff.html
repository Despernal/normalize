normalize.diff.collection_generator(collection)[source]¶

This function returns a generator which iterates over the collection, similar to Collection.itertuples(). Collections are viewed by this module, regardless of type, as a mapping from an index to the value. For sets, the “index” is always None. For dicts, it’s a string, and for lists, it’s an int.
